Ant_footprint/↳

Ant_footprint aims to simulate organisms governed by rules called algorithms. By experimenting with them in an application, these become tangible through a small robotic installation. Each experiment defines a new algorithm that will be executed graphically on the screen and then via the robotic installation. The algorithms are materialised in two ways:

1) Through a graphic result .
2) By the movement produced by the installation.
